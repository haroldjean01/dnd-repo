Harold Jean

As a software engineer and fellow D&D fan, this app was a pleasure to build! My team and I have used other trackers in the past. Our experience in the past gave us precise direction on how we were looking to improve our new app. Features like our dice roll randomizer and status update effects surpasses what we’ve seen in other apps. Other features that have not been seen before is a player’s page. Our players are able to set up all their statistics and add a profile avatar for their character. You can now track all your battles with ease!
